Why Only Conflicts of Interest?

Generally, conflicts of interest are complex issues that are difficult to explain, especially to people who do not have a background in the subject. Unlike many other issues which can be easily discussed in a short news segment, conflicts of interest, and the inter-connectedness of influence in an organization requires more detail which is not generally conducive to major media formats. For this reason, the creation of concise, straight-forward media focusing on this subject is needed in formats that allow for easy sharing and viewing.

With the narrow mission of EyeOn45 Inc., a deeper analysis can be done on this issue, which has mostly been left to traditional journalists. With the new executive administration, there is simply too much material for lone journalists to uncover, digest, and disseminate in a manner that can take hold in the public.

Organization

EyeOn45 has three main branches including research, content creation, and administration. The administration branch handles the inner workings of the organization, drawing in new volunteers, and communicating externally with other similar groups that have already researched these issues. The research branch takes whatever information is already available to the public, verifies information discovered by other groups, and if needed attempts to uncover information as needed for each project. The content creation branch takes the research done and creates various media including pictures, videos, and other web content.

Volunteer for EyeOn45

EyeOn45 is a crowdsourced project so anyone and everyone is welcome to help contribute. The level of volunteering depends only on you! You can contribute as little or as much time as you want. The minimum-investment jobs include submitting any news regarding conflicts of interest to our Reddit group or pledging to share our media when they are released.

Currently, as we are in the initial organization phase, we are in heavy need of graphic designers with either Adobe After Effects, Premiere, Photoshop, or Audition experience, people with business backgrounds that can understand financial documents, lawyers or other people with experience with FOIA requests, and social media representatives including people with Reddit or other moderating experiences.

Please email us or request to join the r/EyeOn45 subreddit to join the team!

Where do donations go?

EyeOn45 Inc. is a charted non-profit organization and will release any and all expenses and other donations received down to the dollar at the end of the year. Donations made to EyeOn45 will be used for the following purposes:

  • Paying for software licenses that allow our creation branch to create more content.
  • Paying for online storage that allows our team members to work collaboratively in remote environments.
  • Paying for any and all media hosting costs.
  • Paying for administrative fees relating to paperwork costs and other tax burdens.
  • Paying for access to media required for creation of new works.
  • Paying for access to information databases needed for research of new works.
  • Paying for legal representation and fees to file FOIA requests if needed.
  • Paying for web hosting costs.
